Web design class questions.

November 16th, 2019

By: Madison Williams

Interview with Jessy McKee

Why do you want to design?

I would like to design some awesome things in the future. Such as kick-ass logos and cool websites and maybe a few books here and there. Who knows I want to do cool things.

What is difficult or frustrating for you within design and how do you work to push past that?

The most frustrating thing designing thing is when the objective isn’t clear. It gets annoying when you create something only to have someone say that that was the total opposite of what they wanted.

What do you see yourself doing after you finish this program?

Honestly I have no clue. Maybe work for some company and go freelance after a while. I think I'll just wing it until I can’t.

What excites you about design?

Honestly, I don’t think anything worries me about it...I’m pretty open-minded about it.

Can design change the world?

I don’t think so...I believe design can impact us in many ways, but I don’t believe that design alone has the power to change the world.

What is a website?

A website is something that you can locate...on the internet that provides all sort of information.
